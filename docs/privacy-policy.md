# Privacy Policy

<p class="legal-updated">Last updated: June 29, 2026</p>

This Privacy Policy explains how **Mantrika Sound** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit **mantrikasound.com** (the "Site") or purchase and use our products, including Mantrika Tools and MTK Offline Render (collectively, the "Products").

By using the Site or our Products, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## Who we are

Mantrika Sound develops audio production tools and extensions for REAPER. If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, you can contact us at **[mantrikasound@gmail.com](mailto:mantrikasound@gmail.com)**.

## Payments and our reseller (Merchant of Record)

Our orders and payments are processed by **Paddle** (Paddle.com Market Limited), who acts as the **Merchant of Record** for all purchases. This means that when you buy a Product, Paddle handles the transaction, billing, and tax compliance on our behalf.

When you make a purchase, your payment details (such as card information and billing address) are collected and processed directly by Paddle — **we never receive or store your full payment card details.** Paddle's handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policy, available at [paddle.com/legal/privacy](https://www.paddle.com/legal/privacy).

## Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the Site, deliver our Products, and provide support:

- **Order and account information** — your name, email address, country, and the products you have purchased. This is shared with us by Paddle to fulfill your order and provide a license.
- **Support communications** — the contents of any emails or messages you send us, so we can respond to your enquiry.
- **Site usage data** — basic, anonymized technical information (such as browser type and pages visited) that may be collected to keep the Site secure and functioning. The Site does not run advertising trackers.

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- deliver your purchase, provide license keys, and enable software updates;
- respond to support requests and communicate with you about your order;
- send important service notices (for example, security or licensing updates);
- comply with our legal and tax obligations; and
- detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse.

## Sharing your information

We share personal information only with parties that help us run our business, including:

- **Paddle**, our Merchant of Record, for payment processing, invoicing, and tax;
- service providers that host the Site or help us deliver software and support; and
- authorities or advisors where required by law.

## Data retention

We keep order records for as long as necessary to provide the Products, comply with legal and tax requirements, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your information at any time (subject to records we must retain by law).

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at **[mantrikasound@gmail.com](mailto:mantrikasound@gmail.com)**. For payment-related data held by Paddle, you can also contact Paddle directly.

## Cookies

The Site uses only the cookies and local storage necessary for it to function (for example, remembering your light/dark theme preference and powering on-site search). Paddle may set cookies during checkout to process your payment securely.

## Children

Our Products are intended for professional and adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

## Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **[mantrikasound@gmail.com](mailto:mantrikasound@gmail.com)**.
